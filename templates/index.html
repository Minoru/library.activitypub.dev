ActivityPub Development

Welcome to a grassroots organized and operated community library for the ActivityPub protocol and Fediverse culture. It aims to be a useful nexus of information both technical and non-technical in nature, containing collection volunteered by people just like you.

Come browse the shelves!

This library's collection has four major categories for capturing the problems, analyses, objections, and technical experiments identified by the community. Additionally, works are tagged to aid in finding interesting literature. All works have primary source links when possible, and if there is no source link provided then it is an original authored work submitted directly to this library!

Your feedback, ideas for improvements, and submissions are always welcome anytime. See how to Contribute or learn more About us. If you are a developer wanting to create an ActivityPub application, or join a community of developers, the SocialHub site is a great resource.

We hope you enjoy your stay.
