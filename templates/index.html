ActivityPub Development

Welcome to a community library on the ActivityPub protocol.

This site documents the problems, analyses, objections, and technical experiments identified by the community. It is intended to be a useful central resource for community members, both technical and non-technical.

This site is a grassroots effort to merely document problems in order to save the sanity of community members who would otherwise be exhausted getting their voice out.

While it could be a resource for those who are making changes to ActivityPub, this site is not the place for that kind of decision-making. By encouraging discussions that directly connect community-identified problems to technical experiments, it is the hope that those who do have the power to draft future versions of ActivityPub refer to this digital library.

Want to learn more? See what this site is About.

Or, check out how to Contribute! An account on the Fediverse is all that is required.
